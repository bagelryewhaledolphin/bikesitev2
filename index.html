Our mission

Students in Princeton often face a lot of academic pressure, which takes away time from exercising and socializing. That's why we created BIKESITE, a website where you can meet new people from Princeton and ride with them around town! We hope this website will encourage you to keep a healthy lifestyle and make some new friends.
